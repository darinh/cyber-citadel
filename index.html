An educational aid that dramatizes NIST SP 800-53 Revision 5 (and the wider Risk Management Framework) as a besieged digital city. On-screen control IDs, titles, and gold quotes are taken verbatim from the official catalog. This is not a substitute for the standard — always consult the NIST publications for real decisions.
